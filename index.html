Architecture: Motif Platform

What it is. Motif is the global content platform behind WBD's editorial brands (Food Network, CNN, Eurosport, Magnolia, Golf Digest, MotorTrend). Loosely coupled Node.js/TypeScript microservices, an AWS-native runtime, and a GraphQL BFF give each brand its own client surface while sharing the platform's content, personalization, and compliance services.

Why this shape. Fault isolation comes from queue decoupling (SNS → SQS → Lambda) so a slow downstream never back-pressures the BFF. The cache-aside Redis layer absorbs read spikes; ElasticSearch handles segment and search; DynamoDB carries hot key-value paths.

Compliance is a first-class service. OneTrust drives GDPR/CCPA user-data access and deletion across every microservice, with auditability baked into the event contracts.
